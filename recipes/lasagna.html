Lasagna Recipe


A simple hearty dish that reheats well. Use a favorite tomato sauce, either homemade or jarred. To make it a really hearty meal, use a bolognese sauce.


Ingredients

  • 1 16 oz package lasagna
  • Olive oil
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten.
  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley (or a tablespoon dried parsley)
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 3 cups marinara sauce or Bolognese sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Prepare the pasta per package directions.
  3. While the pasta cooks prepare the cheese filling.
  4. Combine the eggs, ricotta, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Add 1 1/2 cups of the mozzarella and 1 cup of the parmesan cheese. znd stir to combine.
  6. Toss prepared pasta with a little olive oil to prevent sticking and allow to cool.
  7. Begin layering lasagna in 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
  8. Add some sauce to the bottom of the dish and arrange pasta over the sauce.
  9. Top pasta layer with another 1/2 cup of sauce and 1 cup of the cheese filling.
  10. Continue layering ending with sauce on top.
  11. Spread the remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheeses over the top, cover with foil and place in the oven.
  12. Bake for 30 minutes, remove foil and bake for another 30 minutes until bubbly.
  13. Remove from oven and let cool.
  14. Serve hot with extra sauce if desired.
  15. Enjoy!
  16. Lasagna is almost always better the next day. Reheat in the oven and Enjoy! again
